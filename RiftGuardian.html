What the Project was about

A VR wave shooter made in Unreal Engine. It was a fun project to work on. Most fun part of this is how big the group was. It consisted of 22 People 18 developers and 4 artists. This project lasted for about 2 periods so 6 months With me being elected as one of the lead developers again.

What I did in the project

I began by making the first edition of the shop that was later remade by someone else. At a mid point in the project we realised it was too bland and too straight forward. So we decided to broaden our experience and decided to work on something else. I was part of the team that was reliable to make the 'World' arrow. It was really fun to work on with how many things I was able to work with timelines, materials, scalars, etc. It was also a great learning experience.

Simply put what this does is allowing input actions when it's being held. And what you do for this mapping context is the ability to break it.

When you hold the arrow in 2 hands with the 2 handed script used and made in many other contexts and press Y on the oculus controller it will activate the input action. Which is this.

While this image is not the highest quality due to sizing I can explain what it does. When you break the arrow it will check if you're holding it with 2 hands and if that's true it will play a sound and place 2 halves in your hands

After it's broken you have the ability to stab yourself. That activates this Blueprint

Well starting off once you have stabbed yourself the arrow will check if you are now the parent object and it's being held by the place object.
